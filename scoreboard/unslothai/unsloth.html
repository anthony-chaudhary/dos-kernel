A drift is a commit whose subject claims something its own diff doesn't show — an empty commit that says "fixed it", a "tests pass" that deletes the test. A drift is a message-vs-diff mismatch — never a correctness, honesty, or intent grade. Schema and grade vocabulary: docs/311.
